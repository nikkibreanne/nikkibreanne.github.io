🌿 THE WEEKLY LOOP

One harvest, start to finish — here’s the whole season-week in six sprouts.

  1. Sprout a hero 🌱 Subscribers plant an okra-hero with !create <class>. Your class picks your battlefield job (tank, healer, or damage) and you start with some humble starter gear.
  2. Chat to grow 💬 Every message you send while the stream is live sprinkles EXP on your hero. Hang out, yap in chat, and watch that bar fill.
  3. Level up & gear up ⚒️ Fill the EXP bar to level up and get stronger. When loot drops in chat, !grab to enter the draw, then !equip what you win into your weapon, armor, and trinket slots.
  4. Muster the patch 📣 When the weekly boss is announced, a signup window opens. Subscribers type !muster to enlist your hero in this week’s fight. The roster fills up live on the Muster page.
  5. Raid night ⚔️ At raid night the roster locks and an automated, turn-by-turn battle plays out — your levels, gear, and team balance all decide how it goes. Nobody types during the fight; it resolves on its own.
  6. Watch, loot, climb 🏆 Watch the replay in the Arena, see whether the patch prevailed, and check where your damage landed on the Leaderboard. Then it all begins again next week.

📖 CHAT COMMANDS

Type these in Nikki’s Twitch chat. Commands marked subs are subscriber-only.

  • !create <class> subs
    Plant your okra-hero. Pick one class: Guardian (tank), Mender (healer), Berserker, Arcanist, or Ranger (all damage). You get matching starter gear.
  • !char also: !me
    View your character — class, level, EXP, rating, combat stats, and what you’ve got equipped.
  • !bag also: !inventory, !inv
    Peek at your unequipped loot — everything you’ve grabbed but aren’t wearing yet.
  • !equip <item>
    Wear an item from your bag — it slots into its weapon, armor, or trinket spot automatically.
  • !unequip <slot|item>
    Bare a slot (weapon / armor / trinket) or a named item — it goes back into your bag.
  • !grab subs also: !loot
    Enter the drawing for the active loot drop while the window’s open. One winner is drawn when it closes — the item goes to them. 🎲
  • !muster subs to muster
    During the signup window, enlist your hero for this week’s raid (subscribers). Any time, it also reports the current raid status and links you to the action.
  • !top optional: !top damage
    See the season leaderboard — the patch’s top 5 hardest hitters.

🛠️ Mods have a few extra behind-the-scenes commands for running the show — those aren’t needed to play.

🌟 GOOD TO KNOW

  • EXP only grows while live. Your hero earns EXP from chatting only when the channel is actually live — off-stream messages don’t count.
  • Leveling is steady, not lucky. Each level needs a fixed EXP bar to fill; once it’s full, your level-up lands within a few guaranteed messages. There’s no random jackpot and no bad-luck streak — fill the bar, get the level.
  • Higher sub tiers hit harder. A higher subscription tier boosts both how fast your hero earns EXP and its combat power on raid night.
  • Loot is a fair draw. Every entrant — and every subscriber tier — has the exact same odds of winning the drawing; no tier gets better loot luck than another.

❓ FAQ

I’m subscribed but I’m not gaining EXP — what gives?

EXP only accrues while the stream is live. If the channel is offline, your messages won’t feed your hero — pop back in once Nikki’s live. 🌱

My sub lapsed. Did I lose my hero?

Nope! Your hero, level, and gear are all kept, and you keep earning EXP from chatting while live. You’ll just need to re-sub to create a new hero or to muster for a raid with !muster.
